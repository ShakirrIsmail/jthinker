jThinker
the open-source tool for making TOC diagrams
This is just a brief description of what jThinker can do and how to make him do it. Some additional information can be found on the project's website.
How to create a new diagram?
Pick "File" menu. Choose "new". Select the diagram type you want to create.
How to create a new statement on diagram?
Right click on the diagram pane. Choose what kind of node you want to add (the list of valid node kinds depends on the type of the diagram). Enter the statement.
How to remove a statement?
Right click on the node. Choose "delete".
How to move a statement?
Click on the node. Drag it where you want.
How to connect one statement to another?
Right click on the node. Choose "link". Click on the other node.
How to remove an arrow?
Right click on it. Choose "delete".
How to reverse an arrow?
Right click on it. Choose "reverse".
